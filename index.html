How to play

  • Orbit: drag with the mouse (or one finger).
  • Zoom: scroll wheel (or pinch).
  • Pan: right-drag (or two fingers).
  • Fire: pick a projectile, then click the planet to launch it from the camera.
  • Drop: press Space to release a projectile from high orbit.
  • Pause: press P or the Pause button.
  • Impacts leave permanent craters — use Save to keep your battered world.
